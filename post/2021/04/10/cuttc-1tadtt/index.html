CUTTC - 1tadTT

10 Apr 2021

I wish to start and lead an event among the current and past CUTTC members in memory of Professor Simon Maddrell: CUTTC - 1tadTT: 1 Task A Day Table Tennis for 6 months: 10/04 - 09/10/2021 across Simon’s 1st anniversary on 10/09/2021. To play a game with some one, to do a jog with a TT ball, or carrying a TT bat to get a coffee… all counts!
