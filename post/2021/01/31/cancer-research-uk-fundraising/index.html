Dear my colleagues, friends and family,

It took me nearly 16 years to arrive at 2021 to talk about my family crisis when I lost my mum from Breast Cancer on 5th February 2005, then 8th April my dad passed away from Colon Cancer post their initial diagnosis as late stage cancers in 2003.

On 5th Feb 2021, I had a â€œHead Shaveâ€ in memory of my parents, particularly my mum when she lost her hair due to the chemo/radio therapies. I wish to get more awareness to the difficult daily life of Cancer patients and emphasise the importance of continuing support for researchers amongst out top tasks on the pandemic, hoping there will be fewer and fewer people getting late stage cancer diagnosis whilst still young, and eventually we will Beat Cancer, Together!
